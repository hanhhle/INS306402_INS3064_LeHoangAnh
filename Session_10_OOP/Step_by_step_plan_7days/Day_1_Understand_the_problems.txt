# Day 1 — Understand the problem (no code)
- Write the **requirements list**.
- Identify user roles:
  - Student (creates requests)
  - Staff (updates status)

## Requirements list
### 1. User Roles:
**Student**: Creates and tracks repair requests
**Staff**: Receives, processes, and updates request status

### 2. Requirements List:
- Students can log into the system
- Students can create a new request including: Title, Location (room, building,...) and Description in details
- The system automatically assigns the status Pending to newly created requests
- Students can view a list of their submitted requests
- Students can click on a request to view details
- Students can track the status of their requests in real-time
- Students can receive notifications when the status of their request changes

- Staff can log into the system
- Staff can view a dashboard of all pending requests assigned to them
- Staff can click on a request to view details and update the status
- Staff can view a list of all requests from the entire school
- Staff can filter requests by status (Pending, In Progress, Done)
- Staff can update the status of a request from Pending to In Progress
- Staff can close a request by changing the status to Done

- The system should send notifications to students when the status of their request changes
- The system should maintain a history of status changes for each request
- The system should allow students to delete their requests if they are no longer needed
- The system should have a feedback mechanism for students to rate the service after their request is completed
