# Day 7 — Reflection (bridge to next week’s MVC)
1. Which parts of your design are **Model**, **View**, and **Controller**?
- Model: Request, RequestRepository (database access) and RequestService
- View: Files containing the user interface (HTML/UI) located in the app/Views directory
- Controller: RequestController

2. Where should validation happen, and why?
It should happen in the RequestValidator or at the beginning of the Controller
--> Then immediately block misformatted, empty, or malicious data before it penetrates the system

3. What would break if you put SQL inside a View file?
- Breaks the MVC architecture and SRP principles
- Mix of HTML and SQL can unmaintainable and non-reusable
